[[professional-development]]
=== pass:[<span style="color:#0b3c6f; font-weight:bold;">&#10003; Professional Development</span>]

The Data Mine Corporate Partners program is a professional development program. We want to help you develop the skills you need to be successful in your career. We will have a variety of professional development activities throughout the semester.


Each sprint you will complete a few professional development related tasks. To begin, let's review resumes, virtual meeting expectations, email communications and time management. Please note the combined topic-tasks below pertain to one assignment in Gradescope, called *Sprint {sprint-no}: Professional Development*.


**When:** Professional Development Due on Gradescope by {pd-due}. Late work will not be accepted. 


.*1. Resume Update*
[%collapsible%open]
====

*Why:* Updating your resume is crucial for career fairs because it ensures that your most recent accomplishments, skills, and experiences are highlighted. Presenting a polished resume at the Career Fair will help you get your dream internship/job and make a strong impression on potential employers.

*What:*

    1. Review the {purdue-csh} Make sure to look at the sample resumes on pages 30-36. 
    2. Use the tips you learned to improve to your resume. Include your experience in The Data Mine using these {datamine-guide}.
    - Don't have a Resume? Use {ramped-careers} to generate resume.
    3. Have your resume reviewed with one of the options below: (optional but recommended)
    * {hiration-link}
    * {jobscan-link}
    * {lightcast-link}
    * {cco-resume} via BoilerConnect (limited availability, Purdue students only).
    4. Upload your resume.


====

[%collapsible%open]
====
*Why:* The Data Mine Corporate Partners students meet with their Corporate Partner Mentors online via Microsoft Teams every week. Being able to conduct yourself professionally in a virtual setting is an important skill for students to develop in The Data Mine.

*What:* Please read and watch the video {online-meeting-link} and take the quiz in Gradescope.

.*Email Communication*
[%collapsible%open]
====
*Why:* Effective email communication is crucial in today's professional world. Mastering this skill enhances productivity, demonstrates professionalism, and can significantly impact your career success. By learning how to write clear, concise, and professional emails, you can build stronger relationships with colleagues, mentors, and potential employers. This skill is essential for networking, job applications, and maintaining effective communication in any professional setting.

[%collapsible%open]
====

*Why:* Learning about different time management techniques and effectively incorporating them into you everyday routine helps you optimize your productivity, reduce stress, and achieve a better work-life balance. By understanding various techniques, you can find the methods that work best for your personal and professional needs. 
*What:* You have the option to read about or watch the videos about the following 3 Time Management Techniques. Then, answer the reflection question in Gradescope.
