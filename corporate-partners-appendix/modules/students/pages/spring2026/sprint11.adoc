=== pass:[<span style="color:#0b3c6f; font-weight:bold;">&#10003; Professional Development</span>]

During sprint {sprint-no}, this professional development activity focuses on emotional intelligence and peer feedback as professional skills.


**When:** Professional Development Due on Gradescope by {pd-due}. Late work will not be accepted. 

.**1. Emotional Intelligence**
[%collapsible%open]
====
*Why:* Emotional intelligence is an important professional skill that affects how individuals communicate, collaborate, and manage relationships in academic and workplace settings.

*What:* Watch {ei-video} video. Then complete a written reflection addressing the following:

- Briefly describe the four domains of emotional intelligence discussed in the video.
- Identify one domain you currently do well and one domain you would like to improve.
- Describe one specific professional or academic situation where applying emotional intelligence would improve communication or collaboration.

====

.**2. LinkedIn / Resume Peer Review**
[%collapsible%open]
====
*Why:* Peer review is a common professional practice used to improve communication materials through constructive feedback.

*What:* Review a teammate's LinkedIn profile or resume using Purdue's guidelines ({purdue-ln} or {purdue-resume}). Provide feedback that includes one strength and one suggested improvement, and share it with your peer via email or Microsoft Teams. 
====
