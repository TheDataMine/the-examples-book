= Indy Project Availability

Thanks for your interest in The Data Mine Corporate Partners courses for the spring 2026 semester! This page indicates if projects are willing to accept new students for the upcoming semester. Please check Unitime to see if there are open seats in the course, this page will **NOT** be updated to reflect remaining course capacity.

A few FAQs:

* LEC meetings are 50 minutes. LABs are 1 hour and 50 minutes. 
* Full attendance is required in-person at all class times. 
* The Corporate Partners coures is 3 credits. You must also register concurrently for the 1 credit hour seminar. 
* *Some teams have citizenship restrictions. Click on the links below and then click the project title to see a PowerPoint containing details about the project.*
