=== pass:[<span style="color:#0b3c6f; font-weight:bold;">&#10003; Professional Development</span>]

During sprint {sprint-no}, this professional development activity focuses on building self-awareness by examining imposter syndrome and cognitive biases that influence professional thinking and behavior.

**When:** Professional Development Due on Gradescope by {pd-due}. Late work will not be accepted. 

.**1. Imposter Syndrome**
[%collapsible%open]
====
*Why:* Imposter syndrome is a common professional challenge that can affect confidence, performance, and decision-making. Understanding and recognizing imposter syndrome supports professional growth and self-awareness.

*What:* Watch the provided video {imposter-video}. Then complete a written reflection addressing:

- One key point from the video about imposter syndrome.
- One way imposter syndrome can appear in academic, workplace, or team settings.
- One specific strategy you could use to recognize or manage imposter syndrome in yourself.
====


.**2. Biases**
[%collapsible%open]
====
*Why:* Understanding how cognitive biases and emotional processes shape perception and decision-making improves self-awareness, communication, and professional judgment.

*What:* Watch the provided {bias-video} video to learn how the brain's default patterns influence thinking and behavior. Then complete a written reflection on Gradescope addressing:

- One example of a common cognitive or emotional bias described in the video.
- How that bias might appear in academic, workplace, or team interactions.
- One specific strategy you could use to recognize or counteract that bias in yourself.
====
