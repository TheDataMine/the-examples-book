[[professional-development]]
=== pass:[<span style="color:#0b3c6f; font-weight:bold;">&#10003; Professional Development</span>]

During sprint {sprint-no}, this professional development activity focuses on project closure and team reflection at the conclusion of a year-long project.

**When:** Professional Development Due on Gradescope by {pd-due}. Late work will not be accepted. 

.**1. Project Closing**
[%collapsible%open]
====
*Why:* Preparing for project closure is an important professional skill. This activity helps you think intentionally about project handover and closure while practicing professional communication through writing thoughtful thank-you letters to those who supported your work.

*What*: Review the provided articles on project handover and project closure, as well as guidance on writing meaningful thank-you letters. Apply these readings by responding to the provided prompts about your project and by writing a thank-you letter addressed to your mentor(s) and to your TA or a Data Mine staff member.

====

.*2. Tuckman's Review*
[%collapsible%open]
====
*Why:* Understanding team development helps you recognize how teams evolve over time and how collaboration, conflict, and performance change across different stages. Reflecting on your team’s experience supports self-awareness and prepares you for future team-based professional work.

*What:* Review the provided article {team-development}. Then complete a written reflection addressing how your team progressed through the Forming, Storming, Norming, Performing, and Adjourning stages over the course of your year-long project, using the provided prompts.
====
