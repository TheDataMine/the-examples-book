== Charty Party

Similar to Apples to Apples or Cards Against Humanity​

* Distribute 7 orange "y-axis" cards to each player​

* Pick a "judge" for the round​

* Judge displays one white "chart" card and ​describes what is happening in the chart​

* Each player (but the judge) chooses ONE orange card from their hand to play, placing it face down by the white card in the center​

* The judge then picks their favorite orange card and the round resets with that player as the new judge​

* Players may pick up new orange cards to total 7​

== 10 Similarities

Students work in teams to find 10 things they have in common.


* Divide your team into groups of 2-5 people.

*  Teams will have a set amount of time to identify 10 things everyone shares.

** No limits to scope, it can be personal, work related, or cultural.

** Must ask each other questions.

** Teams can not do negatives.


* When the time is up, teams can come back together and share out the most interesting thing they have in common.
